Md Shakil Ahamed Shohag is a master degree student at University of Jinan, China. He completed his bachelor in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Development Alternative, Bangladesh. He is working as a research assistant in Shandong Provincial Key Laboratory of Network Based Intelligent Computing, University of Jinan, China. His research interests include Computer Vision, Image Processing, Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Graph Matching. He has previously worked in wireless networking, mobile Ad-hoc network security and web security.
